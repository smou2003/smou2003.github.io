About Us

The Carnegie Mellon Computer Graphics & Animation Club (CGA) aims to provide a support network for those interested in various aspects of the 2D and 3D animation pipelines.

Our goals are as follows:

  1. To help connect students interested in animation and/or computer graphics with other students, faculty, professionals and other resources in the aforementioned fields
  2. To create a larger presence for animation at CMU, and give students who are interested in the topic the chance to connect with and collaborate with other people involved in animation
  3. To bring in alumni and professionals in the industry as speakers, periodic workshops on technical skills
