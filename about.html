About Us

Hi! We're a club at Carnegie Mellon University dedicated to computer graphics and animation!

Events and resources we offer:
Workshops
Hour-long tutorials on a specific software or skill
Open to anyone, regardless of skill level!

Industry talks
Presentations/talks with alumni and professionals in the animation industry

Semester project
Complete a personal project with support from us
Our (optional) theme this year: Underwater!
We have weekly work sessions (optional) to facilitate collaboration (and to provide a fun, productive environment!)

Socials
We're a friendly community! Come meet new friends and fellow animators!
