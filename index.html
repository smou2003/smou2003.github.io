Computer Graphics & Animation!

We are Carnegie Mellon University's very own computer graphics and animation club!

We aim to bring students from all studies interested in any kind of computer graphics and animation into one place to spark innovation, creativity, and excitement for the digital arts.

Join us for workshops, industry talks, collaborative animation projects, and socials!
